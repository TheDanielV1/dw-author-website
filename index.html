Daniel Vendramini

Author | Student | Triplet

Daniel Vendramini is an up and coming author, refusing to let go of his passion for storytelling. A self-described nerd who in his own time enjoys crafting DnD campaigns. Daniel is currently studying a Bachelor of Arts (Creative Writing) at RMIT.

Consistenly described as "mclovin from superbad" by his friends, Daniel is a beloved figure in his social circles, known for his quick wit and infectious laughter.
